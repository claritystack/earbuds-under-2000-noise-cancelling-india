FAQ

Do earbuds under ₹2000 really have noise cancelling?

Most offer passive noise isolation or entry-level ANC. Performance varies by fit and design.

Is ANC worth it under ₹2000?

At this price, ANC is usually entry-level. A good fit and passive noise isolation often make a bigger difference.

Which earbuds under ₹2000 are best for voice calls?

Look for models with ENC and multiple microphones designed for call clarity.

Are budget earbuds good for traffic noise?

Strong ear tip sealing combined with passive isolation can significantly reduce traffic and background noise.
